GYPSUM BOARD AS BUILDING MATERIALS

Gypsum boards, also known as drywall, plasterboard, or wallboard, are panel products consisting of a noncombustible gypsum core sandwiched between paper facings. These versatile building materials are widely used in modern construction for various applications, including wall linings, partitions, sound control, and fire protection.
